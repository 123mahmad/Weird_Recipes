Christmas Candle Salad Recipe.......?

Image Credits: Vintage Recipe Cards

Description

This one has crossed the line of weirdness into something? Seven bananas on gelatin molds with a little dab of mayonnaise on top of each banana. Clearly, the creator had a goal in mind

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 envelopes ( 1 1/2 tablespoons) unflavored gelatin
  • 1/3 cup cold water
  • 2 cups canned cranberry juice cocktail
  • 4 ripe bananas
  • 8 salted almonds
  • Mayonnaise
  • Salad greens

Steps

  1. Soften gelatin in cold water.
  2. Heat cranberry cocktail to boiling; add to gelatin and stir to dissolve gelatin.
  3. Pour into 8 small, star-shaped molds.
  4. Chill until firm; unmold. Cut out and remove a small circle from center of each star the same diameter as the bananas.
  5. Peel bananas; cut in halves, crosswise. Insert 1/2 banana in center of each of each star as shown.
  6. Top each “candle” with a salted almond for a “flame”. Add a little mayonnaise to look like melted wax.
  7. Serve on salad greens with mayonnaise.

Servings

Makes 8 servings.
